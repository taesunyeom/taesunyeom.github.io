Taesun Yeom

I'm a second-year M.S. student at EffL (Efficient Learning Lab), POSTECH (advisor: Prof. Jaeho Lee).

My recent research focuses on understanding generalization in deep learning from a theoretical perspective. More specifically, I am interested in the following research questions:

  • How do nonlinear feature learning dynamics emerge during neural net training, and how do they shape generalization?

  • Can we rigorously quantify the generalization benefits of widely used training methods, such as knowledge distillation?

Before joining EffL, I received my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Chung-Ang University and worked closely with Prof. Minhyeok Lee and Prof. Seokwon Lee.
